Forsaken:

Forsaken is a game where there is 8 survivors and 1 killer.
Your role is to try to survive for 3/4 minutes, but doing generators takes 3.5 seconds from the timer(per puzzle)If the killer kills one person,
it gives him another 30 seconds(apart from lms).
But its not just running away. Certain survivors
called "sentinels" have certain abilities to help them fight back!
Its a thrilling game and it is action packed!


Rivals:

Rivals is a first person shooter experience and is described as
"The most intense fps game on Roblox!
PLayers spawn in a lobby where they can battle each other, shop
for items and leaderboards. When you load into a game, you have a loadout
that consists of 4 items:Primary,Secondary,Meelee and Utility.
Its the perfect game for someone who is looking for a FPS game with no blood details, and its thrilling!


99 Nights in The Forest:

99 Nights in the Forest is a horror/Survival game where you have to save 4 kids in the duration of 99 nights.There are "classes" that are bought with gems to give you certain perks. (Not real nights)
Even if you don't manage to save all of them, you still win, your goal is to just survive.
However, there is a mutant deer, owl and ram trying to stop you!
Overall, It is a thrilling game that is not too scary.
